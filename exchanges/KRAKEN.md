- **CFTC $1.25M Penalty** (Sept 2021): unregistered margin trading. Resolved via Bitnomial acquisition.

### Security Track Record:
- **Zero breaches resulting in loss of client funds since 2011** (15 years)
- Pioneered Proof of Reserves in 2014 — first exchange to do so
- Merkle tree user-level verification
- Quarterly independent auditor confirmation
- All reserve ratios above 100% (BTC 104%, ETH/USDT/USDC >100%)
- Cold storage for vast majority of funds
- Bug bounty program, 2FA passkeys, PGP email encryption, API key range boundaries

## Ad Strategy Verdict

Kraken is the **most likely exchange to already be advertising on ChatGPT** due to:
1. Heavy sports sponsorship spend (F1, FIFA World Cup, Premier League, La Liga)
2. Deliberate paid marketing phased rollout (search → social → brand plays)
3. Pre-IPO brand awareness push (S-1 filed)
4. CMO with performance marketing DNA (ex-Spotify)

Their ChatGPT ad sweet spots:
- **Security/trust** — the zero-breach story is unmatched and highly relevant to ChatGPT users researching "which exchange is safe"
- **CFTC-regulated US perps** — massive product launch, regulatory first, strong press hook
- **Stocks + crypto convergence** — appeals to traditional investors asking ChatGPT about crypto
- **Krak** — consumer fintech angle no exchange has, appeals to mainstream users

**Key risk:** SEC lawsuit (ongoing). If ChatGPT pulls from recent news, this could surface in ad context. Kraken's ads should lead with "15 years, zero breaches" — a fact the SEC lawsuit can't undermine.
