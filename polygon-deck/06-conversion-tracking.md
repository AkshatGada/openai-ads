# What ChatGPT Ads Let You Do

---

## Built-In Conversion Tracking

ChatGPT Ads has a native conversion measurement system. You place a small tracking snippet on your landing page, and the platform tracks every action — page visits, button clicks, form submissions. This is not a third-party integration. It's built into the ad platform itself.

Here's how it works for OMS:

1. Someone asks ChatGPT about stablecoin payment infrastructure. Your context hint matches. Your ad appears.
2. They click the ad and land on your early access page or docs.
3. They read, evaluate, and click "Get Early Access" or submit the form.
4. ChatGPT Ads logs the conversion — from impression, to click, to form submission — in a single, connected pipeline.

You see exactly how many people came from which context hint, which ad creative they saw, and whether they converted.

---

## Why This Matters for an Early-Stage Product

OMS is in technical preview. You're not optimizing for direct revenue — you're optimizing for qualified leads: waitlist signups, early access requests, developer onboarding. ChatGPT Ads gives you a closed-loop measurement system to track that exact funnel.

- **Track visits**: How many people reached your landing page from each ad group
- **Track form submissions**: How many of those visitors submitted the early access form
- **Track which context hints convert**: Identify which personas and intents are driving real leads, not just clicks
- **Optimize spend**: Shift budget to the hints and creatives that generate signups, pause the ones that don't

This means your $50/day test budget isn't a black box. Within the first week, you'll know which context hints are bringing qualified fintech PMs and marketplace CTOs to your early access form — and which are bringing tire-kickers.

Your conversion destination can be:

- **https://info.polygon.technology/get-early-access** — the existing early access form
