## Why Claude Can't Do This

An LLM can generate context hints. It can draft ad copy. What it cannot do is tell you whether those hints will actually trigger an ad, which competitors will appear alongside you, or what CPC you'll pay against real bidders. Those answers don't live in a language model. They live in the live ChatGPT auction — and we built the infrastructure to measure it.

Our scraper probes ChatGPT continuously with thousands of prompts across categories, capturing every ad that appears. That data feeds our analysis engine, which processes hundreds of thousands of data points to map which signals trigger ads, which companies are bidding on them, and where the empty lanes are. No LLM has access to this dataset. We collected it ourselves.

## How We Optimize

- Build context hints that describe the exact persona and their intent, verified against live probe data before a dollar of spend
- If a hint isn't getting impressions, we broaden or refine it
- If a competitor enters the auction on your signal, we alert and adjust before your CPC rises
- If an ad gets clicks but no conversions, we rotate creative against the same hint
- Active optimization loop — not a set-and-forget campaign
