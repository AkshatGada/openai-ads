# Personas, Prompts & Ad Creative

## Persona 1: The Fintech PM

**Who**: Product lead at a neobank, told to add stablecoin payments. Never owned crypto. Needs compliance approval.

**What she asks ChatGPT**:

> *"My CEO wants us to add stablecoin payment rails to our neobank. But our legal team vetoes anything crypto-related. Is there a regulated API that handles fiat deposits, USDC transfers, and bank off-ramps — without our users ever seeing a token or a wallet?"*

**The OMS ad she'd see**:

> **Add Stablecoin Rails. Never Mention Crypto.**
> Regulated fiat ramps, auto-wallet creation, KYC built in. One REST API. Your users see dollars, your compliance team sees an audit trail.

## Persona 2: The Bank VP

**Who**: Payments lead at a regional bank. 20 years in traditional finance. Board is pushing for blockchain settlement. He's skeptical.

**What he asks ChatGPT**:

> *"Our board wants us to explore blockchain-based settlement. We move about $200M in cross-border wires monthly. Is any of this actually running at institutional scale? I need production SLAs, SOC 2, audit trails. Not a pilot — production."*

**The OMS ad he'd see**:

> **Enterprise Stablecoin Settlement. In Production.**
> $54B processed. 159M wallets. SOC 2. RBAC. SSO. 6 years in production across Revolut, Stripe, and Flutterwave infrastructure.

## Persona 3: The Marketplace CTO

**Who**: CTO of a marketplace paying 5,000 sellers globally. Losing $200K/year on FX and wire fees. Doesn't care about blockchain — cares about the P&L.

**What he asks ChatGPT**:

> *"We pay 5,000 sellers across 40 countries every month using a mix of PayPal, Wise, and bank wires. We're losing about $200K a year in fees and settlement takes days. Is there a stablecoin payment API that converts to USDC, settles instantly, and off-ramps to sellers' bank accounts? They shouldn't need a crypto wallet."*

**The OMS ad he'd see**:

> **Pay Sellers Globally. They Get Dollars.**
> One API for deposits, USDC settlement, and bank off-ramps. $0.002 per transaction. 48 US states. No crypto wallets required.
